关于 JOIN ARTAbout Join Art

Join Art 国际艺术教育位于英国伦敦。作为英国本土艺术教育机构，Join Art 将结合中西方艺术教育的优势，为学生提供线上及线下的教育支持。

团队致力于为申请海外顶尖艺术院校的学生提供最新的专业信息，提供一站式、全方位的海外艺术培训与规划服务。

Based in London, Join Art is a premier UK art institution. We offer holistic training for students aspiring to world-class art schools by combining Eastern and Western pedagogies.

Our team provides one-stop, comprehensive overseas art training and planning services.
